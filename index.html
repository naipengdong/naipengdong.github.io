About Me

I am an academic researcher and senior lecturer at the University of Queensland, specialising in formal verification, AI security, post-quantum cryptography, and privacy analysis. I lead and collaborate on multiple industry-linked research projects, including QDSA and AEA Ignite, focusing on quantum-safe systems, AI-driven security verification, blockchain and secure software ecosystems.
